Privacy Policy — Eternal Bhagavad Gita

Effective date: 21 October 2025 · Developer: Anand R. · Contact: artektrend@gmail.com

Eternal Bhagavad Gita is a devotional/educational app presenting the Bhagavad Gita in Sanskrit with transliteration, translation, meanings, search, audio playback, and a verse-of-the-day feature. We do not require user accounts and we do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Data We Collect

  • No account, login, or profile.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • No location, contacts, camera, or microphone access.

Local App Storage

The app may store non-personal preferences on your device (e.g., a one-time snackbar “thank you” flag) using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Device Permissions

  • Network: to load remote content and donation pages.
  • Audio playback: to play chapter verse audio. The app does not record audio.

Payments / Donations

The Donate button opens an external payment page (e.g., PayPal). Donations are voluntary and do not unlock digital goods or features. Part of the payments are donated to charitable organizations. Payment processing is handled by the external provider; we do not receive or store your payment details. Please review their privacy policy when donating.
